Three services that work as well together as they do apart. Pick one, or let us bundle them into a single project.

Website & web app builds

  • — Fully responsive, tested from 320px up

  • — Accessible markup: labels, alt text, keyboard nav

02 · security

Security audits & hardening

Manual testing first — the kind of poking around an automated scanner misses — backed by standard tools to catch the rest. You get a report ranked by real-world severity, not a wall of low-priority warnings.

Built for teams who need to know what's actually exploitable before launch, not just a checklist marked "pass."

03 · care plan

Ongoing monitoring

Security isn't a one-time stamp. Care plan clients get uptime checks, dependency updates, and a full re-scan every quarter — so the site that passed its audit in January isn't quietly out of date by June.
