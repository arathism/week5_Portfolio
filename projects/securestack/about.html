cat about.md

Built by people who ship and people who break things

SecureStack started as two freelancers who kept getting hired twice for the same project — once to build the site, once to fix it after a scare. Putting both jobs under one roof was the obvious fix.

mission

Ship fast without shipping holes

Small businesses are told to choose: launch quickly, or launch safely. We don't think that trade-off should exist for a five-page business site. Every build goes through the same security checklist we'd run for a paying audit client — no separate "basic" tier that skips it.

We keep teams small on purpose. The developer who wrote your contact form is the same person who tests it for injection before it goes live, so nothing is lost translating between a build team and a security team that never talk to each other.
